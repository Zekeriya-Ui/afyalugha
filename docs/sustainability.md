# Sustainability Plan

## Post-Grant Strategy (Year 2+)

Afyalugha is designed for long-term sustainability through a diversified revenue model, open-source community growth, and capacity building in coastal Kenya.

---

## Revenue Model

| Revenue Stream | Year 2 | Year 3+ | Notes |
|----------------|--------|--------|-------|
| Government contracts (Kenya MoH) | $50,000 | $50,000 | Digital health strategy alignment |
| NGO partnerships | $30,000 | $30,000 | AMREF, MSF, IRC health programs |
| Clinic SaaS subscriptions | $20,000 | $20,000 | Tiered pricing: public clinics subsidized |
| Research grants | — | $100,000+ | Follow-on Lingua Africa; NIH Fogarty; Wellcome |
| **Total** | **$100,000** | **$200,000+** | |

### Pricing Philosophy

- **Public health facilities:** Heavily subsidized or free (cross-subsidized by NGO contracts)
- **Private clinics:** Cost-recovery pricing based on patient volume
- **Research institutions:** Data licensing fees for commercial use of corpus
- **Core open-source model:** Always free; revenue from hosted services and support

---

## Capacity Building

### Local Developer Training (Months 9–12)

- Train **5 local developers** in Swahili NLP techniques
- 3-month structured program covering:
  - Transformer model fine-tuning
  - Medical NLP and annotation
  - Deployment and monitoring
  - Evaluation methodology
- Graduates form the core team for Year 2 operations

### Knowledge Transfer

- Full technical documentation for system operation and maintenance
- Tutorials published on GitHub and Masakani Africa
- Annual Swahili NLP workshop at a Kenyan university (target: University of Nairobi)

## Open Source Community

### Afyalugha Open Source Ecosystem

All core resources released under permissive licenses to ensure the work outlasts the project:

| Resource | License | Community Use |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| Swahili Medical Corpus | CC BY 4.0 | Researchers, health organizations worldwide |
| NER Model | MIT | Developers building Swahili health apps |
| Chatbot Code | AGPL 3.0 | Open deployment with attribution |
| Evaluation Benchmark | CC BY 4.0 | Academic benchmarking |

### Community Growth Targets

- Year 1: 500+ dataset downloads; 50+ GitHub stars
- Year 2: 2,000+ dataset downloads; 5 derivative projects
- Year 3: African NLP community standard for Swahili medical NLP
